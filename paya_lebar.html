Welcome to Paya Lebar

Paya Lebar is a bustling interchange station connecting the Circle Line with the East West Line. Historically a rural swamp, it has evolved into a thriving commercial hub rich in cultural heritage.

Local Highlight

Paya Lebar is home to the Geylang Serai Market & Food Centre, the cultural heart of the Malay community, offering famous local food such as Nasi Lemak, Putu Piring, and Otah-Otah.
